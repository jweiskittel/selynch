Landscape Maintenance

Behind every great looking landscape is an equally viable maintenance program. S.E. Lynch Landscape Artistry can tailor a customized landscape maintenance program to fit your home or business and keep your property in top form for years to come.

Our maintenance services may include:

  • Spring and Fall Bed Cleanup
  • Mulching and Bed Edging
  • Pre-Emergent Weed Control and Removal
  • Annual Seasonal Color Programs
  • Lawn Mowing and Trimming
  • Lawn and Ornamental Fertilization
  • Ornamental and Shrub Pruning
  • Leaf Removal
  • Lawn Core Aeration
  • Snow Removal and Ice Melting
