Landscapes

S.E. Lynch Landscape Artistry is well-versed in the design and implementation of many different styles of softscape installation. With your overall look in mind, we can deliver a planting package that complements your existing landscape or gives you a fresh new look.

Our softscape solutions may include:

  • Foundation Plantings
  • Rock Gardens
  • Perennial Beds
  • Privacy and Screen Plantings
  • Mounded Planting Islands
  • Shade and Ornamental Tree Plantings
  • Patio and Deck Accent Plantings
