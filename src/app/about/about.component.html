The S.E. Lynch Design Process

1. It's All About You

At S.E. Lynch Landscape Artistry, we realize you have many options in selecting a landscape firm. Our success in this industry comes from knowing our clients and building lasting relationships. We value your input and vision to help achieve the best possible solution for your selected project. We want to be your landscaper for life. Our design professionals will meet with you at your home or business to discuss your landscaping goals, view the site and most importantly, listen to your project wishes.

2. Your Canvas. Our Artistry.

Before execution of your landscape plan, digital photos of your property are taken to catalog every detail. A scaled landscape drawing is initiated, complete with all labeled features and a cost estimate is done based on the design. A presentation appointment is set up between you and our design professional for explanation, feedback and revisions if necessary. Once both parties agree on the design and proposal, a contract is signed and the project is scheduled.

3. The Finished Product

Once execution of your project begins, we will work diligently, without interruption, to complete the job in a timely manner. You can be sure every detail is implemented as planned. When your job is complete and to your satisfaction, a final walkthrough is scheduled. The project is now yours to enjoy.
